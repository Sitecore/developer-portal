title: 'SitecoreAI Pathway'
---
*SitecoreAI Pathway* is an AI-powered toolset that assists in migrating the pages of your XM sites to the target structure of your new *CMS* site (formerly called *XM Cloud*). The toolset is accessed from the [Sitecore Marketplace](/sitecoreai/sitecore-studio/marketplace). Please contact your Sitecore account representative to get started.

This page hosts downloads that are implemented as pre-requisites to running *SitecoreAI Pathway*.

Resource | Description
--- | ---
[XM Component Extraction](https://scdp.blob.core.windows.net/downloads/SitecoreAI_Pathway/XMComponentExtraction-1.0.0.zip) | Tooling and instructions for extracting page content from your source XM sites. Includes a handler that you install on your source XM instance and a console app to manage the extraction. 
[CMS Export Structure](https://scdp.blob.core.windows.net/downloads/SitecoreAI_Pathway/CMSExportStructure-1.0.0.zip) | Script and instructions for generating a JSON file describing the target structure of your CMS site. This file is used by the AI-powered tooling in the mapping of the source XM page designs to the target CMS site structure.
