Route high-risk content—pricing, medical, regulatory claims—to **full review**. Auto-approve only when confidence, policy checks, and duplicate detection all pass. Surface model **rationales** as bullet points so reviewers scan faster than raw paragraphs.

Measure **time-to-approve** and calibrate staffing against seasonal campaigns.
