Start with **explicit goals** (conversion, engagement, self-service resolution) and map each to measurable signals. AI layers should consume the same normalized event stream your rules engine trusts—avoid shadow pipelines that drift.

Document **consent tiers** so recommendation models never train on data the visitor did not authorize.
