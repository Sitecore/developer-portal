Copilots land fastest when they **shorten** repetitive work: title variants, alt text proposals, and compliance checklists. Full-page generation is higher risk—pair it with mandatory human review queues and diff views against the published baseline.

Track **acceptance rate** per suggestion type; retire prompts that rarely get adopted.
